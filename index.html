About

I am a Cloud Computing Platform performance and security researcher focusing on investigating the cloud computing infrastructure performance, modelling, analysis and testing as well as performance vshttps://dbi.edu.ng/ security trade-offs evaluation. Current research centres on the development of machine learning models for performance and security analysis in a cloud computing environment using production datasets. At the same time served as a teaching assistant at the University of Bradford, participated in developing, delivering and assessing different computer science modules at both undergraduate (UG) and postgraduate levels. Prior to that, I have been a lecturer of computer science at Usmanu Danfodiyo University, Sokoto (UDUS) – Nigeria. Moreover, I have participated in a collaborative research project between IBM – USA, DBI – Nigeria & UDUS – Nigeria where we successfully initiated, planned, designed and developed a prototype application ‘Answers for Farmers’ which aimed at assisting farmers with information using a basic mobile phone. I am also an associate fellow (AFHEA) of UK higher education academy , with years of experience in an academic environment in research, teaching as well as managing and coordinating educational sessions in collaboration with colleagues and all the stakeholders such as Network administrators, IT staff. During my PhD research from the University of Bradford in the United Kingdom under the supervision of Professor Demetres Kouvatsos, Dr Mumtaz Kamala and Dr Mariam Kiran. My research contributed to the core network research and design of the UK major university based cloud computing facility using comodity hardware known as (Bradstack).

Research Interest

My research is focused on design and development of machine learning models for performance vs security analysis using a Cloud Computing Platform's production datasets.. My research interest lies at the intersection between network systems, control systems, and machine learning..

My current research interest include:

  • Developing ML models for Cloud performance and security analysis

  • Performance modelling and analysis of Virtualisation in Cloud Computing Platforms

  • Network Function Virtualisation

  • Software Defined Networking

  • Cloud Platform implementation (Openstack, AWS, Microsoft Azure, Google Cloud Platform)
